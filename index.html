DISCLAIMER

This is not an official name generator, nor is it associated with the developers, Digital Extremes.

Quality of names is not guaranteed. It is highly encouraged to take inspiration from the generated names and spin-off from them.

Notes

  • Where is there Steel Meridian/Perrin Sequence option for character name generation? Since names for the Perrin Sequence and Steel Meridian (generally) follow Corpus and Grineer conventions respectively, for character names they are omitted to avoid redundancy.
  • Corpus roles are drawn from the Skaut air support charge and is non-exhaustive
  • Railjack crewmate flavor text has been made gender-neutral; all texts are sourced from the Warframe wiki.

Special thanks to sunnytries for collecting Corpus roles as seen in-game, and to space-ninja-fashion-show and many others for assistance with data collection on names.
